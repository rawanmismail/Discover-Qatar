Adventures Await in Qatar

Desert

Desert Safari and Dune Bashing

Experience the dramatic landscape of the Qatari desert on an exhilarating 4x4 dune bashing adventure. Experienced guides lead you through towering sand dunes, with stops for sandboarding, camel riding, and traditional Bedouin camp experiences beneath a sky blazing with stars. The Inland Sea at Khor Al Adaid is the iconic destination for desert adventures.

Water Sports

Adventures on the Arabian Gulf

Qatar's warm, calm Gulf waters are perfect for a wide range of water sports. Jet skiing, kayaking, paddleboarding, and parasailing are available at beach resorts along the coast. Scuba diving and snorkelling offer access to rich coral reefs and diverse marine life. Sailing and dhow trips provide a more leisurely way to experience the Gulf.

Sporting Events

World-Class Sporting Venues

Qatar's sporting calendar is packed with major international events. The Khalifa International Stadium hosts athletics meetings and football matches. Horse racing at the Qatar Racing and Equestrian Club runs from October to May. The Doha Golf Club, home to the Qatar Masters, offers championship-level play for visiting golfers.

Activities

More Ways to Stay Active

Golf

Championship courses at Doha Golf Club and Education City Golf Club welcome visitors of all abilities.

Horse Riding

Equestrian centres offer lessons, desert treks, and beach rides in stunning settings.

Running

The Corniche and dedicated parks offer popular routes; regular parkrun events welcome all abilities.

Fitness Centres

World-class gyms, CrossFit boxes, and sports academies operate across the country.

Cycling

Dedicated cycling tracks and group rides are available in Doha and surrounding areas.

Swimming

Hotel pools and beach resorts offer exceptional swimming facilities throughout the year.
