Sun and Sea

Qatar's Best Beaches and Resorts

The Pearl

The Pearl-Qatar

Qatar's most famous man-made island is a resort destination in itself. The Pearl-Qatar features several kilometres of beautiful beaches, luxury residences, boutique hotels, marinas, and a spectacular array of dining and shopping options. Dedicated beach clubs offer sun loungers, water sports, and full resort amenities on the island's pristine shores.

Learn More
Sealine

Sealine Beach Resort

Located in Mesaieed, approximately 45 minutes south of Doha, Sealine Beach Resort offers a classic Gulf beach experience. Sandy beaches, calm waters, water sports facilities, and access to the stunning desert dunes of the nearby Inland Sea make this a favourite destination for both residents and visitors seeking a complete beach and desert combination.

Beach Activities

Water Sports and Beach Fun

Sailing

Sailing excursions and yacht charters explore the beautiful coastline of the Arabian Gulf.

Snorkelling

Rich coral reefs and diverse marine life await snorkellers in the clear Gulf waters.

Jet Skiing

Jet ski rental is available at major beach resorts and water sports centres.

Fishing

Deep sea and traditional fishing trips are available from several locations along the coast.

Beach Clubs

World-class beach clubs offer sun loungers, pools, restaurants, and full resort amenities.

Scuba Diving

Certified dive centres operate throughout the country, catering for all experience levels.
