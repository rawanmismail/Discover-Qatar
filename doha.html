Capital City

Exploring Doha

Doha is the beating heart of Qatar, a city in perpetual motion that offers world-class culture, dining, shopping, and entertainment.

Corniche

The Iconic Doha Corniche

Stretching seven kilometres along the western shore of Doha Bay, the Corniche is the city's most beloved public space. Walk, cycle, or simply sit and admire the stunning views of the West Bay skyline reflected in the azure waters of the Gulf. The Corniche is the perfect starting point for any exploration of the capital.

Souq Waqif

Souq Waqif — The Living Market

One of Qatar's most beloved landmarks, Souq Waqif is a beautifully restored traditional market where the scent of spices fills the air and the sounds of everyday Qatari life surround you. Browse stalls selling everything from traditional garments and handcrafted jewellery to exotic birds and aromatic oud. Dozens of restaurants serving authentic regional cuisine line the atmospheric alleyways.

Culture

World-Class Museums

Doha has established itself as a global capital of culture, home to some of the world's finest museums. The Museum of Islamic Art, designed by I.M. Pei, houses one of the greatest collections of Islamic artefacts on earth. The National Museum of Qatar, with its stunning desert rose architecture, tells the story of Qatar's history and natural environment.
