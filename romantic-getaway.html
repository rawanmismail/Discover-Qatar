For Couples

Romance in the Heart of Arabia

Sunset Dhow

Sunset Dhow Cruise

Sail into the sunset aboard a traditional wooden dhow as the Doha skyline transforms into a palette of gold and rose. Sip refreshments, savour a private dinner on deck, and watch the city lights reflect on the shimmering waters of the Gulf. Sunset dhow cruises are among the most romantic experiences Qatar has to offer.

Learn More
Desert Dining

Dinner Under the Stars

Experience the magic of the Qatari desert with a private dinner set amongst the dunes, illuminated by candlelight and the vast desert sky. Leading tour operators offer fully styled tables, gourmet menus, and live traditional music, creating an extraordinarily intimate experience far from the city.

Learn More
Luxury Spa

Couples Spa Retreats

Qatar's luxury hotels offer world-class couples spa experiences drawing on ancient Arabian wellness traditions. Hammam treatments, aromatic oil massages, and private bathing experiences in stunning surroundings create the perfect romantic interlude. Several resorts offer dedicated couples pavilions with total privacy.
