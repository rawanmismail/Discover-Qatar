Arriving in Qatar

Your Journey Starts Here

By Air

Qatar Airways — World's Best Airline

Qatar Airways, the national carrier, is consistently ranked among the world's finest airlines and serves as the primary gateway to the country. Operating from the award-winning Hamad International Airport, Qatar Airways connects Doha to over 160 destinations worldwide with a modern fleet and exceptional service standards.

Connections

Global Connectivity

Beyond Qatar Airways, numerous international carriers operate direct services to Doha, including British Airways, Lufthansa, Emirates, and Singapore Airlines. The central geographic location of Qatar makes it an ideal hub for connections between Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas, typically requiring no more than one stop from virtually anywhere in the world.
