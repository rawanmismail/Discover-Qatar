Welcome to Hamad International Airport

The Airport

An Airport Like No Other

Hamad International Airport (IATA: DOH) is one of the most remarkable airports in the world. Handling tens of millions of passengers annually, it offers a seamless blend of art, architecture, retail, dining, and hospitality. The iconic Lamp Bear sculpture by Swiss artist Urs Fischer welcomes arriving passengers, while the airport's stunning interiors feature works by internationally acclaimed artists.

Facilities

World-Class Passenger Facilities

Whether you have a long transit or are simply passing through, Hamad International Airport offers outstanding facilities. The Al Mourjan Business Lounge is regularly cited as one of the finest airport lounges globally. Passengers can enjoy a wide selection of dining, retail, a swimming pool, hotel, spa, squash courts, and even a 25-metre indoor running track.

At the Airport

HIA Services and Facilities

Duty Free Shopping

An extensive range of luxury brands, local products, electronics, and duty-free goods.

Dining

Over 30 restaurants and cafes offering everything from Qatari cuisine to international favourites.

Airport Hotel

The Oryx Airport Hotel offers comfortable rooms for passengers requiring overnight stays.

Spa and Wellness

Relax with a treatment at the spa or take a dip in the airport swimming pool.

Family Facilities

Dedicated family lounges, play areas, and nursing rooms ensure comfort for travelling families.

Ground Transport

Metro, taxis, and pre-booked transfers are readily available outside the arrivals terminal.
