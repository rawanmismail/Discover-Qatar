Best Time to Visit

When to Come to Qatar

Winter Season

October to April — Peak Season

The cooler months between October and April offer the most comfortable climate for outdoor activities, sightseeing, and events. Temperatures typically range between 15 and 25 degrees Celsius, making this the ideal time to explore deserts, beaches, and the city on foot. This period also coincides with Qatar's busiest events calendar, including sporting championships and cultural festivals.

Learn More
Summer Season

May to September — Off Peak

Qatar's summer months bring intense heat, with temperatures regularly exceeding 40 degrees Celsius. However, this is also when hotels offer their most attractive rates and the country is less crowded. Indoor attractions, shopping malls, and air-conditioned cultural venues remain fully accessible and enjoyable throughout the summer months.
