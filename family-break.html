For Families

Qatar's Best Family Experiences

Theme Parks

Angry Birds World and IMG Worlds

Qatar is home to some of the region's most exciting theme parks. Angry Birds World at Doha Festival City offers thrilling rides and interactive experiences based on the beloved characters. The country's growing portfolio of entertainment attractions ensures families have no shortage of options for exciting days out.

Learn More
Beach & Nature

Beaches and Nature Experiences

Qatar's safe, calm Gulf beaches are ideal for families with young children. Al Khor Beach and the beach clubs at The Pearl-Qatar offer safe swimming areas, water slides, and full resort facilities. Nature lovers can explore the Al Thakira Mangroves on guided kayak tours, spotting flamingos and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

Learn More
Education

Educating and Entertaining

Qatar's world-class museums are designed with families in mind. The National Museum of Qatar features interactive galleries that captivate children and adults alike. The Children's Museum of Qatar is dedicated entirely to young learners, with hands-on exhibits exploring science, art, and Qatari culture.
