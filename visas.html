Visa Options for Qatar

Qatar has significantly simplified its visa requirements in recent years, making it easier than ever for international visitors to enter the country. Whether you are planning a short holiday, a business trip, or a stopover, there is a visa option to suit your needs.

Visa-Free Access

Nationals of over 100 countries can enter Qatar without a prior visa, receiving a free visitor visa on arrival valid for 30 days, extendable for an additional 30 days. Eligible nationalities include most European countries, the United States, Canada, Australia, and many Asian nations.

Hayya Card and e-Visa

Visitors from countries not on the visa-free list can apply for a visa online through the Hayya platform or the Qatar e-Visa portal. The process is straightforward and typically results in a decision within 72 hours. An e-Visa typically allows a stay of up to 30 days.

Transit Visa

Passengers transiting through Hamad International Airport can apply for a free transit visa valid for up to 96 hours, allowing them to explore Doha before continuing their journey. This is an excellent way to discover Qatar without a dedicated holiday.

Entry Requirements

All visitors must hold a passport valid for at least six months from the date of entry. Return or onward tickets are required, as is proof of accommodation. Travel insurance, while not mandatory, is strongly recommended.

Visa at a Glance

  • Visa-Free: 100+ countries
  • Duration: 30 days (extendable)
  • Cost: Free on arrival
  • e-Visa: Available online
  • Transit: Up to 96 hours free
