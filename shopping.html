Shopping in Qatar

From traditional souqs overflowing with spices and handcrafts to gleaming luxury malls, Qatar is a world-class shopping destination.

Shop Qatar

The Best Shopping Experiences

Traditional

Souq Waqif and Traditional Markets

Souq Waqif is the quintessential Qatari shopping experience. Navigate its atmospheric alleyways to discover stalls selling traditional garments, handcrafted silver jewellery, aromatic oud perfumes, colourful spices, hand-woven rugs, and authentic Arabic antiques. The Falcon Souq — one of the few remaining in the world — is a unique and fascinating attraction for visitors.

Learn More
Luxury

Luxury Shopping Malls

Doha's modern shopping malls offer an extraordinary concentration of luxury brands. The Mall of Qatar, Qatar's largest mall, houses over 600 stores including every major international luxury brand alongside a full entertainment complex. Villaggio Mall with its Venetian-inspired interior and Place Vendome with its stunning atrium are further highlights of Doha's retail landscape.

Learn More
Unique

Unique Shopping Experiences

The Pearl-Qatar offers boutique shopping in a stunning waterfront setting, with independent designers, artisan studios, and concept stores set alongside the marina. Msheireb Downtown Doha features a new generation of independent retailers in a beautifully designed sustainable urban environment. Regular pop-up markets and craft fairs add further dimension to Qatar's shopping landscape.

Shopping Guide

Key Shopping Destinations

Souq Waqif

Traditional market with handicrafts, spices, perfumes, textiles, and the famous Falcon Souq.

Mall of Qatar

Qatar's largest mall with 600+ stores, a theme park, cinema, and dining destinations.

The Pearl-Qatar

Boutique shopping with luxury brands and independent retailers in a waterfront setting.

Villaggio Mall

Iconic Venetian-inspired mall featuring premium international brands and leisure facilities.

Place Vendome

A sophisticated luxury mall inspired by Parisian elegance with high-end fashion and dining.

Festival City

A vast retail and entertainment complex featuring department stores and family attractions.
