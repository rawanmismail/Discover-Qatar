Wellbeing

Qatar's Premier Wellness Experiences

Hammam

The Traditional Hammam Experience

The hammam — a steam bath ritual with deep roots in Ottoman and Arab culture — is one of the most authentic wellness experiences Qatar has to offer. Guests undergo a purifying steam treatment followed by an invigorating black soap scrub and a revitalising massage. Several luxury hotels and dedicated spa facilities offer traditional hammam experiences of the highest quality.

Luxury Spas

World-Class Hotel Spas

Qatar's luxury hotels are home to some of the most extraordinary spa facilities in the world. The Six Senses, ESPA, and Canyon Ranch concepts operate in top properties, offering comprehensive menus of treatments ranging from deep tissue massage to signature Arabian ritual therapies. Many spas include hydrotherapy pools, saunas, ice rooms, and relaxation lounges as standard.

Yoga

Mindfulness and Yoga

A growing number of dedicated wellness studios and hotel wellness programmes offer yoga, meditation, and mindfulness sessions throughout Doha. Beachside sunrise yoga, rooftop meditation sessions, and comprehensive wellness retreats provide opportunities to achieve balance and inner calm alongside the more physical activities Qatar has to offer.
