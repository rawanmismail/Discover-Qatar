Sports Nation

Qatar — A Premier Sports Destination

Football

The 2022 FIFA World Cup Legacy

The 2022 FIFA World Cup transformed Qatar's sporting landscape. Eight state-of-the-art stadiums, including the iconic Lusail Stadium, were built or renovated. The tournament attracted over a million visitors and left a legacy of world-class facilities that continue to host major international events.

Learn More
Traditional Sports

Falconry, Camel Racing and Dhow Sailing

Beyond international sports, Qatar cherishes its traditional sporting heritage. Falconry championships draw enthusiasts from across the Gulf. Camel racing at Al Shahaniya track is a spectacular experience. Dhow sailing competitions celebrate Qatar's maritime heritage in the waters of the Gulf.

Learn More
Athletics

World Athletics Championships

Qatar successfully hosted the 2019 World Athletics Championships in Doha, further cementing its status as a serious global sports hub. The Khalifa International Stadium, one of the finest athletics venues in the world, continues to host major international competitions.

Active Qatar

Sports and Activities for Visitors

Golf

Championship golf courses set in stunning locations offer world-class play for golfers of all abilities.

Water Sports

The warm Gulf waters are perfect for diving, snorkelling, jet skiing, kayaking, and sailing.

Horse Racing

Qatar Racing and Equestrian Club hosts thrilling flat racing events throughout the cooler months.

Fitness

World-class gyms, tennis academies, and sports complexes are available throughout Doha.

Cycling

Dedicated cycling paths along the Corniche and in suburban parks offer scenic rides for all levels.

Running

Organized running events and parkrun sessions attract participants of all abilities across the country.
