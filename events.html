Annual Highlights

Key Dates in Qatar's Calendar

October

Cooler weather returns, the football season begins, and Qatar's events calendar kicks into high gear.

November

Major international sporting championships and the start of the outdoor festival season.

December

Qatar National Day celebrations, the DSF shopping festival launches, and festive events throughout the country.

February

Horse racing season in full swing, tennis tournaments, and the Qatar International Food Festival.

March

The spring season brings outdoor concerts, art exhibitions, and the Qatar Science and Technology Park exhibition.

April

The last comfortable month before summer; outdoor festivals and cultural events wrap up the cool season.
