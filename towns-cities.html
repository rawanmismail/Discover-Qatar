Why Doha is the Heart of Qatar

Doha

A City Like No Other

Doha is one of the fastest-growing cities in the world, yet it has managed to preserve its cultural identity amidst dramatic modernisation. The West Bay skyline is one of the most recognisable in the Middle East, while the historic Souq Waqif and the Corniche waterfront offer a gentler, more traditional side of the capital.
